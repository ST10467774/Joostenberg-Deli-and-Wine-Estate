Our Story

Family roots, seasonal cooking and wines made on the farm.

We believe in honest food, sustainable farming and warm hospitality.

Short History

Klein Joostenberg forms part of the historic Joostenberg estate near Stellenbosch. What began as a family kitchen garden and small butchery has grown into a bustling deli, bakery and restaurant, supported by our own herds, orchards and vineyards. The deli celebrates local producers, traditional charcuterie and country baking, while our wines are crafted with a light touch to express the farm’s granitic soils and cool breezes.

Today, families gather under the oaks for breakfast and lunch, stock the pantry with farm produce, and take home our Joostenberg wines—made right here on the property.

Philosophy

Cook seasonally, source locally, waste less. Our farm-to-table approach keeps ingredients fresh and flavours honest.
